About Institute

NIT Polytechnic is an AICTE-approved institute offering diploma programs in engineering and technology. The institute focuses on practical knowledge, innovation, and professional development.

The campus includes digital classrooms, modern laboratories, library facilities, and student development centers aimed at holistic growth.
